Welcome to NBPAM

NBPAM (Nation Builders Prophetic Apostolic Movement) provides care, support and a safe haven for elderly persons in the Kouga & Sarah Baartman District. Established in 2018, our mission is to restore dignity and improve quality of life for older persons through community-based services.

Our Services

Multipurpose Centre for Elderlies

  • Active ageing programs and daily support services
  • Meals-on-wheels and nutrition programs
  • Transport to clinics and community events
  • Skills development and recreation

Hope & Healing Safe House

  • Daily caregiving and hosting
  • Health support and counseling
  • Protection and rehabilitation for victims of abuse

Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide professional care that restores dignity and promotes quality of life for older persons.

Vision: To act transparently and effectively within South African law, building partnerships to support vulnerable seniors.
